Academics Programme and Curriculum Structure

The Academic session at Police Modern School, Harsaon, Ghaziabad starts from April to March. The School provides education to students from Nursery to XII.
Police Modern School, Harsaon, Ghaziabad is a Coed English medium Day School which offers the National curriculum CBSE, from Nursery to XII.
