About School


Police Modern School, Ghaziabad, is a Senior Secondary School (XI-XII), affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). The School is a Coed Day School, with classes from Nursery to XII. It is an English Medium school. The school is located in Ghaziabad. Police Modern School, was established in 1995. It is a Society and is part of Police Modern School and is managed by Uttar Pradesh Police Shiksha Samiti. Checkout some facts about Police Modern School.

The School

Police Modern School provides an opportunity for an all-round education. It has the security of rich tradition under the aegis of the Trust and an ethos conducive to developing each child's potential to the fullest.

Vision

The school maintains a sharp focus on the pursuit of knowledge and skill, in particular, to the attainment of the highest academic standards. The school aims at broader development of the complete personality of its students

Our Belief

At Police Modern School, we believe that the best education is a life-long journey that gives courage, builds resilience, helps to achieve personal excellence and fosters giving.

Our Mission

The mission of Police Modern School is to facilitate joyful learning by linking Indian culture with education and empower the learners to meet the challenges at global level.

Affiliation

The school is a co-educational English medium public school affiliated to the CBSE, Delhi, for the All India Secondary School Examination (X), and the All India Senior School Certificate Examination (XII)..
